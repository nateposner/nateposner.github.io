About Us

Perched high atop the mountain in a beautifully remodeled, repurposed fire lookout, Rising Sun Café offers more than just a cup of coffee—it offers an experience. With panoramic views that stretch as far as the eye can see, we’re the perfect destination for those seeking a tranquil escape or an adventurous reward after the climb.

Our café is a celebration of history and nature. Once a vigilant watchtower protecting the forest below, this historic building has been lovingly transformed into a cozy haven for coffee enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. Inside, you’ll find warm wood accents, a welcoming atmosphere, and a menu crafted to elevate your day.

We pride ourselves on serving ethically sourced, small-batch coffee alongside a selection of fresh pastries, hearty breakfast bites, and local treats. Whether you’re here for a sunrise brew, a midday pick-me-up, or to wind down at sunset, every sip and bite comes with an unforgettable view.

At Rising Sun Café, we believe in honoring the past, embracing the present, and inspiring every visitor to see the beauty that surrounds us. Come visit us at the peak—where great coffee meets greater heights.
